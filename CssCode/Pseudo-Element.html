According to insights from the New York Times, Apple's top executives concluded last year that Siri requires a major overhaul to remain competitive. This realisation was prompted by the capabilities demonstrated by AI chatbots like OpenAI's ChatGPT in handling a wide range of tasks. Particularly, the ability to understand context in language, enabling users to pose vague queries and still receive accurate responses, was deemed a significant advancement. Sources familiar with the matter noted that Apple is actively working on integrating AI capabilities into Siri.
